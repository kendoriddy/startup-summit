"Innovate For Africa!"

ENTREPRENEURSHIP AND
STARTUP SUMMIT
2022

A summit where investors, entrepreneurs, and tech leaders from all over Africa huddle together for two days of intense learning and networking in Abuja, Nigeria.

2022.10.25(THU) ~ 26(FRI)

@ National Event Center, Garki Abuja, Nigeria

Main Program

Lecture

Listen to the speakers with diverse experience about entrepreneurship and running of startups.

IFA Exhibition

Share ideas and acknowledge ideas with the potential of making positive impact in the society.

Forum

Have the opportunity to share your thoughts and opinions with experts for each topic.

Workshop

Put your ideas into action and create awesome MVPs, rather than just watching.

IFA Ignite

Get opportunity to network with IFA advocates around the world and find lifelong mentors.
